---
title: What ties them together
description: The shared thread across the Vertex Network of indie tools.
---

These sites are independent products, but they share a philosophy: **focused tools, free at the surface, monetized only where it makes sense.** No accounts when accounts aren't needed. No ads on tools you'll use for ten seconds. No newsletters for the sake of it.

Every site in the network:

- **Loads in under a second.** Static-first wherever possible. Layout is reserved before content lands so nothing jumps.
- **Respects your privacy.** Cookieless analytics by default. Microsoft Clarity is consent-gated. No third-party tracking inside calculator logic.
- **Tells you when it last updated.** If a site shows you fee or pricing data, it shows you the date that data was last verified.
- **Owns its own domain.** No `subsite.parentbrand.com`. Each tool stands alone.
- **Links to its siblings without burying them.** The "Part of the Vertex Network" footer link is small but always there. The `/network` page lists every site, with the current one omitted automatically.

If a tool here is useful to you, a related one probably is too. That's the only reason this network exists.
