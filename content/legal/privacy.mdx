# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: {{last_updated}}_

This Privacy Policy describes how **{{site_name}}** ({{site_url}}) handles information when you use the site. {{site_name}} is operated independently as part of the Vertex Network.

## What we collect

**By default, nothing.** {{site_name}} is a tool, not an account-based service. You can use the entire site without signing in, providing an email, or otherwise identifying yourself.

The site uses the following limited data flows, each disclosed below:

### Analytics

- **Vercel Analytics** — anonymous, cookieless page-view counts. No cross-site tracking. Vercel's privacy policy: https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy
- **Microsoft Clarity** — session recordings and heatmaps. Loads **only after you accept the cookie banner**. Microsoft's privacy notice: https://privacy.microsoft.com

### Optional services {{#if email_enabled}}

- **Resend** (email capture) — if you choose to subscribe to updates, your email address is forwarded to Resend, which manages our mailing list. Resend's privacy policy: https://resend.com/legal/privacy-policy

{{/if}}

{{#if pro_enabled}}

- **Stripe** (payments) — if you upgrade to a paid tier, payment processing is handled by Stripe. {{site_name}} never sees your full card number. Stripe's privacy policy: https://stripe.com/privacy

{{/if}}

{{#if ads_enabled}}

### Advertising

- **{{ad_provider}}** — third-party ads may be served on this site. The ad provider may set cookies for ad personalization. You can opt out of personalized advertising at https://www.aboutads.info/choices.

{{/if}}

## What we don't do

- **No selling data.** We don't sell, lease, or transfer information about visitors to anyone.
- **No cross-site tracking on tool inputs.** Whatever you enter into a calculator or generator stays in your browser unless you explicitly share or save it.
- **No fingerprinting.** No device fingerprinting libraries; no canvas/font fingerprinting.

## Cookies

{{site_name}} uses cookies only for:

1. Remembering your cookie-banner choice (so we don't ask you again).
2. Loading Microsoft Clarity (only if you accepted).
3. Remembering preferences you explicitly set (e.g., theme, saved scenarios) — stored in `localStorage`, not cookies.

## Your rights

You can clear all site data at any time via your browser's site settings. There is no account to delete.

If you have a privacy question, email **{{contact_email}}**.

## Changes to this policy

If we materially change how data is handled, we'll update this page and the "Last updated" date above. Substantive changes will also be noted in the [/changelog]({{site_url}}/changelog).
